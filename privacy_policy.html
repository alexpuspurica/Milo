Section 01

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how your Workout Tracker App ("the App," "we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and safeguards your personal information. We take your privacy seriously: all data you enter or import into this App stays on your device and is never shared with any third party, sold, or transmitted to external servers.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Section 02

Data We Collect

The App collects only the data you actively provide or choose to import:

2.1 Manually Entered Workout Data

  • Exercise names, sets, reps, and weight
  • Workout duration and date/time
  • Personal notes or comments on sessions
  • Custom routines and exercise templates you create

2.2 WHOOP-Imported Data

If you choose to connect and import data from your WHOOP device, the following data may be accessed and stored locally on your device:

  • Recovery scores and HRV (heart rate variability)
  • Strain scores and workout activity data
  • Sleep performance and sleep stage data
  • Heart rate and respiratory rate readings
  • Calories burned and other biometric metrics provided by WHOOP

WHOOP data is imported directly into the App and stored only on your device. We do not access your WHOOP account credentials, and we do not retain or relay any WHOOP data to our own servers.

Section 03

How We Use Your Data

All data collected by the App is used solely to provide you with the App's core functionality:

  • Displaying your workout history and progress over time
  • Calculating performance metrics and trends
  • Correlating WHOOP recovery and strain data with your workout logs
  • Generating personal summaries and charts within the App

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, analytics reporting, or any purpose beyond what you can directly observe within the App.

Section 04

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored exclusively on your local device. Specifically:

  • No data is uploaded to external servers or cloud infrastructure operated by us
  • Data is stored in the App's private, sandboxed local storage — inaccessible to other apps on your device
  • If you use a device backup service (e.g., iCloud or Google Backup), your App data may be included in that backup, subject to that service's own privacy policy

We recommend you keep your device and operating system updated to take advantage of the latest security protections.

Section 05

Third-Party Services

5.1 WHOOP Integration

The App integrates with the WHOOP API to allow you to import your biometric data. Your interaction with WHOOP is governed by WHOOP's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. We encourage you to review those documents at whoop.com.

When you connect WHOOP to this App:

  • You authenticate directly with WHOOP — we never see or store your WHOOP username or password
  • We only request the minimum WHOOP data permissions necessary for import
  • You may disconnect WHOOP at any time from within the App

5.2 No Other Third Parties

We do not integrate any advertising SDKs, analytics platforms, crash reporting services, or other third-party data processors. The App does not contain trackers of any kind.

Section 06

Data Sharing

🔒
We do not share your data with anyone — period.

Because all data is stored locally on your device, we are technically unable to access or share it even if we wanted to.

This includes:

  • No sale of data to data brokers or advertisers
  • No sharing with partners, affiliates, or subsidiaries
  • No disclosure to government or law enforcement (we hold no data to disclose)
  • No anonymized or aggregated data sharing
Section 07

Your Rights & Controls

Because your data lives entirely on your device, you have complete control:

  • Export: You may export your workout data from within the App at any time
  • Delete: Deleting the App from your device permanently removes all locally stored data
  • WHOOP disconnect: You may revoke the App's access to your WHOOP account at any time through the App or directly in your WHOOP account settings
  • No account required: The App does not require you to create an account, so there is no profile to delete
Section 08

Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us so we can assist with deletion.

Section 09

Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will notify you within the App and update the Effective Date at the top of this document. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
