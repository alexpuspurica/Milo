Section 01

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the Milo Workout Tracker ("the App," "we," "our," or "us") handles your data. Milo is a web application built with Streamlit and hosted on Streamlit Community Cloud. It helps athletes track workouts and receive machine-learning-based training recommendations.

We collect only what is necessary to run the app. We do not sell your data, build advertising profiles, or share information with third parties beyond what is described in this policy.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Section 02

Data We Collect

2.1 Workout Data You Enter

  • Exercise names, sets, reps, and weight
  • Planned vs actual performance per session
  • Weekly training schedule and custom routines
  • Session dates and completion status

2.2 Recovery Data

  • Manual recovery score (1–10) entered by you each session
  • If you connect WHOOP: recovery score, HRV, strain, and sleep data imported from your WHOOP account

WHOOP data is fetched directly from the WHOOP API using your authorisation and is used only within your current session. We do not store WHOOP data on any server we operate.

2.3 Technical Data

Streamlit Community Cloud, our hosting provider, may collect standard web server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) as part of normal infrastructure operation. We do not control or access this data directly. See Streamlit's privacy policy for details.

Section 03

How We Use Your Data

All data you provide is used solely to deliver the App's core features:

  • Displaying your planned and completed workouts
  • Running the machine learning model to generate a weight-increase recommendation
  • Showing progress charts and planned vs actual comparisons
  • Populating the exercise library via the wger API

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics reporting, model retraining, or any purpose beyond what you can directly observe in the App.

Section 04

Data Storage & Hosting

Milo is a web application hosted on Streamlit Community Cloud. This means:

  • Workout data you enter is stored in your browser session and in a local SQLite database on the server running the app
  • If you use the publicly hosted version, your session data lives on Streamlit's infrastructure and is subject to Streamlit's Privacy Policy
  • If you run the app locally on your own machine, all data stays entirely on your device
  • We recommend running locally if you want full control over your data

For maximum privacy: clone the repository and run streamlit run app.py on your own machine. No data leaves your device in this configuration.

Section 05

Third-Party Services

5.1 Streamlit Community Cloud (Hosting)

The public version of this app is hosted on Streamlit Community Cloud. Streamlit may process data as part of serving the application. Please review Streamlit's Privacy Policy for full details.

5.2 wger Exercise API

The App sends search queries to the wger REST API (wger.de) to populate the exercise library. Only the text of your exercise search is sent — no personal data is transmitted. wger is a free, open-source fitness platform.

5.3 WHOOP API (Optional)

If you choose to connect WHOOP, the App requests access to your recovery, strain, and sleep data via the WHOOP OAuth 2.0 API. When you connect WHOOP:

  • You authenticate directly with WHOOP — we never see or store your WHOOP username or password
  • We request only the minimum data permissions needed
  • You can disconnect WHOOP at any time from the Settings page or directly in your WHOOP account settings
  • Your interaction with WHOOP is governed by WHOOP's Privacy Policy

5.4 No Advertising or Analytics SDKs

We do not include any advertising networks, analytics platforms, crash reporting services, or tracking pixels. The App contains no third-party trackers beyond the services listed above.

Section 06

We do not sell or share your data.

This includes:

Section 07

Your Rights & Controls

  • Run locally: Clone the repo and run the app on your own machine — no data leaves your device
  • Clear session: Closing the browser tab ends your Streamlit session and clears session-state data
  • Delete logs: You can delete individual workout logs or your full history from within the App
  • Disconnect WHOOP: Revoke access at any time through the Settings page or directly in your WHOOP account
  • No account required: The App does not require registration or login — there is no account profile to delete
Section 08

This App is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has used this App and provided personal data, please contact us so we can assist.
